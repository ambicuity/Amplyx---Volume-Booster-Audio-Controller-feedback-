# Amplyx Support

## Get help

- Main support channel: [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/ambicuity/Amplyx---Volume-Booster-Audio-Controller-feedback-/issues)
- Start here for a new request: [Open New Issue](https://github.com/ambicuity/Amplyx---Volume-Booster-Audio-Controller-feedback-/issues/new/choose)
- Email contact (security/sensitive concerns): contact@riteshrana.engineer

## What to open

- **Report a bug:** include browser version, extension version, site URL, and steps to reproduce.
- **Request a feature:** share your use case, expected behavior, and impact.
